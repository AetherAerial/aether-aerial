About AETHER AERIAL

Vision elevated beyond the horizon

Aether Aerial is a premium drone cinematography brand built for clients who refuse to compromise on visual excellence. Independently operated, veteran owned, and built on a commitment to precision — we operate under Part 107 certification with professional-grade DJI hardware, delivering imagery that transforms perspective — whether for commercial real estate, branded content, events, or narrative film.


Every flight is a cinematic mission. Every frame, deliberate. From pre-production planning to final color grade in DaVinci Resolve, we control the entire visual pipeline so the story arrives exactly as intended.

Process

How we operate

01
Discovery & Pre-Flight

We learn your vision, scout locations remotely, verify airspace (Aloft, B4UFLY, FAA TFR), and obtain all necessary authorizations before a single motor spins.

02
The Flight

Precision piloting with cinematic intent. Every shot angle is purposeful. We fly methodically to capture the full shot list — and the unexpected moments between.

03
Post-Production

DaVinci Resolve color pipeline from log to delivery. We grade to the look and feel of your project — raw, cinematic, neutral, or stylized.

04
Delivery

Final assets delivered in your preferred format, resolution, and codec — typically within 48 hours. Revisions handled with precision and speed.
